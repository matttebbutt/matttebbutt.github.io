What we do

The UK Work & Support Service (UKWSS) is responsible for employment, welfare, pensions and child maintenance policy. As one of the UK's largest public service organisations, it administers the State Pension and a range of working age, disability and ill health benefits to around 22 million claimants and customers.

UKWSS is a ministerial department, supported by 12 agencies and public bodies.
